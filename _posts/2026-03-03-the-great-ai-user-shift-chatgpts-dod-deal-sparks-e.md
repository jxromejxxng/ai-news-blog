## The ChatGPT Exodus: Users Ditch for Claude Amid DoD Deal

This past week saw a pretty dramatic shift in the AI chatbot landscape. Following news of OpenAI's deal with the Department of Defense (DoD), many consumers started ditching the ChatGPT app in droves. We're talking about a whopping 295% surge in uninstalls! [AI News & Artificial Intelligence | TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/02/chatgpt-uninstalls-surged-by-295-after-dod-deal/)

It seems users are reacting to OpenAI's transition from a wildly successful consumer startup to a piece of national security infrastructure. The company, quite frankly, seems a bit unequipped to manage these new, complex responsibilities, leading to questions about trust and how AI companies should even work with governments. [AI News & Artificial Intelligence | TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/02/openai-anthropic-department-of-defense-war-hegseth-ai-companies-work-with-us-government/)

So, where are people going? Many are flocking to Anthropic's Claude. If you're considering making the switch yourself, it's pretty straightforward, and there are plenty of guides out there to help you migrate. This shift highlights that for many, the 'who' behind the AI and 'what' they're doing with it matters just as much as the tech itself. [AI News & Artificial Intelligence | TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/02/users-are-ditching-chatgpt-for-claude-here-s-how-to-make-the-switch/)

## Even Claude Has Its Off Days: A Quick Reality Check

While Claude is certainly having its moment in the sun, it's important to remember that no tech is perfect. Earlier this week, Claude experienced widespread service disruptions, with thousands of users reporting issues accessing the bot. [AI News & Artificial Intelligence | TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/02/anthropics-claude-reports-widespread-outage/)

The practical takeaway here? Always have a backup plan, especially if you're relying on these tools for critical work. Diversifying your AI tools or having manual processes in place can save you a lot of headaches when the inevitable happens.

## AI Takes Over Customer Support: Meet 14.ai

On the more practical, business-focused side, we're seeing AI continue to make significant inroads into everyday operations. A company called 14.ai, founded by a married duo, is making waves by directly replacing customer support teams at startups. [AI News & Artificial Intelligence | TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/02/a-married-founder-duos-company-14-ai-is-replacing-customer-support-teams-at-startups/)

They've even launched a consumer brand to really push the boundaries of how much AI can handle in customer support tasks. For businesses, this means potential for massive efficiency gains and cost savings. For those in customer support roles, it's a clear signal to adapt, upskill, and focus on the complex, human-centric problems that AI can't (yet) solve.
